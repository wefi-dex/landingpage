Frequently Asked Questions

Have any Question?

What kind of businesses do you work with?

We work with small and mid-size businesses across industries: retail, tech, logistics, and professional services. Whether you need strategy, operations support, or digital growth, we tailor our approach to your goals and stage.

How do you start a new engagement?

We begin with a free consultation to understand your challenges and objectives. From there we propose a scope, timeline, and way of working. You can choose project-based work or ongoing support depending on what fits your business best.

Do you offer ongoing support after a project?

Yes. Many clients move from a one-off project to ongoing support for strategy, operations, or execution. We can agree on a retainer or regular check-ins so you keep momentum and adapt as your business grows.

How can I get in touch or request a quote?

Use the contact form on this page, or email us at contact@happyvic.com. We reply within one business day and will set up a call or meeting to discuss your needs and next steps.
