FRINK PROJECT SNAPSHOTS
=======================

Put your 5 Frink screenshots here so they show on the Projects page.

Required filenames (exact):
  frink-1.png   - Send Money
  frink-2.png   - Features (Proof-of-Person)
  frink-3.png   - Buy or Sell Frinks
  frink-4.png   - Friends
  frink-5.png   - Earn Frinks

How to add them:
1. Locate your 5 Frink screenshots (e.g. in Downloads or wherever you saved them).
2. Copy or move them into this folder:  public/projects/frink/
3. Rename each file to match the names above (frink-1.png through frink-5.png).
4. Refresh the projects page in your browser.

If you no longer have the files, paste the 5 Frink images into the chat again and save them to your PC, then copy them here with the names above.
